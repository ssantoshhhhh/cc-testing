About Campus Connect

Making academic resources accessible to every student on campus

Our Mission

Campus Connect was created to solve a common problem faced by engineering students - the need for specialized tools like mini drafters and lab aprons that are often expensive and only needed for specific projects or limited periods.

By providing a rental service within the campus, we help students save money, reduce waste, and ensure everyone has access to the tools they need to succeed in their studies.
